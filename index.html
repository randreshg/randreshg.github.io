Research Direction

Compilers, runtime systems, and AI infrastructure should speak the same semantic language.

My research centers on execution contracts: explicit declarations of State, Dependency, and Effect that make scheduling-relevant semantics compiler-visible. The goal is to reduce the amount of distributed orchestration, asynchronous overlap, and workflow coordination that developers must encode manually.

In practice, that means co-designing LLVM/MLIR compiler pipelines, OpenMP and event-driven runtimes, and typed agentic workflow systems that improve latency, utilization, correctness, and developer productivity across HPC and AI-native workloads.
